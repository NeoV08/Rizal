Who was José Rizal?

José Protacio Rizal Mercado y Alonzo Realonda (1861–1896) was a Filipino nationalist, reformist, physician, and writer whose life and works inspired the Philippine Revolution. He was an intellectual beacon who envisioned freedom and dignity for his people. Through his writings, civic projects, and moral leadership, he helped awaken the Filipino nation.
